# 01 Intake and Qualification

Use this workflow to capture lead submissions, enrich profiles, and decide whether to proceed to scheduling.

- **Trigger**: Webhook or form submission.
- **Enrichment**: Add CRM lookups or data providers.
- **Decisioning**: Define rules that determine if the lead should be scheduled.
